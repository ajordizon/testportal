My Investments


Your employer pays pension contributions on your behalf. These pension contributions are invested. In that way you accrue a pension capital. We call this an investment pension. There are different ways of investing your contributions, involving more or less risk. Your risk profile indicates which investment style suits you best. You also have the possibility of purchasing guaranteed pension.

Your Risk Profile


Your risk profile indicates that a Neutral investment style suits you best. It also indicates that you should reduce your investment risk to 0% after your retirement date.
